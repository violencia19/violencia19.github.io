She Affirms

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

She Affirms ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the She Affirms mobile application (the "App"). We designed She Affirms with privacy at its core — your personal data stays on your device.

1. Overview

She Affirms does not collect, store, or process any personal information on external servers. All of your data — including mood entries, favorite affirmations, and streak history — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

The App does not require user registration, account creation, or login. You can use She Affirms without providing any personally identifiable information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored on Your Device

She Affirms uses Apple's SwiftData framework to store the following information locally on your device:

  • Mood entries and check-in history
  • Favorite affirmations
  • Streak and usage progress data
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. If you delete the App, this data is permanently removed.

2.2 Anonymous Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect basic, anonymous usage data to help us improve the App experience. This includes:

  • General app usage patterns (e.g., screens viewed, features used)
  • App performance and crash data
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics

Firebase Analytics does not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information. Analytics data is associated with anonymous identifiers and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.3 Purchase Information

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlements. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt data from Apple — it does not receive your name, email, or Apple ID. We do not have access to your payment information, billing details, or Apple ID.

2.4 Information We Do Not Collect

For absolute clarity, She Affirms does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other device data
  • Browsing history
  • Health or biometric data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous data we receive is used exclusively to:

  • Understand general app usage patterns to improve features and content
  • Monitor app stability and fix technical issues
  • Manage subscription entitlements and access to premium features
  • Make informed decisions about future app development

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than improving the She Affirms experience.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to share.

Anonymous, aggregated analytics data processed by Firebase Analytics is governed by Google's privacy practices. Subscription transaction data is processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat as described in Section 4.

6. Data Security

Your personal data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. We do not transmit or store your personal data on any external servers.

While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we believe that local-only data storage provides the strongest practical protection for your privacy.

7. Data Retention

On-device data: Your local data persists on your device until you delete the App or manually clear the data. We have no access to this data and cannot delete it on your behalf.

Analytics data: Anonymous analytics data is retained by Firebase Analytics in accordance with Google's data retention policies. We configure Firebase Analytics to retain data for the minimum period necessary.

Subscription data: RevenueCat retains anonymous subscription records as necessary to manage entitlements. Apple retains purchase records in accordance with their privacy policy.

8. Your Rights

8.1 General Rights

Because She Affirms stores data locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the App
  • Manage app preferences and stored data within the App's settings
  • Limit analytics collection by adjusting your device's privacy settings

8.2 European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to:

  • Access — Request information about any data associated with you
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of data associated with you
  • Restriction — Request limitation of data processing
  • Portability — Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection — Object to data processing

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, these rights are inherently satisfied. If you have any concerns, please contact us at the address below.

8.3 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act grants you the right to:

  • Know — Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected
  • Delete — Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-Out — Opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-Discrimination — Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

She Affirms does not sell personal information. Because we do not collect personally identifiable information, these rights are inherently satisfied. You may still contact us with any privacy-related requests.

8.4 Opting Out of Analytics

You can limit analytics data collection by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" or disabling "Share Analytics" in your iOS device settings under Settings > Privacy & Security.

9. Children's Privacy

She Affirms is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has somehow provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to address the matter.

The App is designed for and marketed to adults, primarily women aged 18 and older.

10. International Users

She Affirms is available internationally and supports multiple languages. Regardless of your location, your personal data remains stored locally on your device. Anonymous analytics data may be processed in the United States or other countries where Google and RevenueCat operate. By using the App, you acknowledge this processing.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and notify you through the App or the App Store update notes.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
