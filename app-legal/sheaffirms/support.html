Common questions

Where did my streak go?

The streak resets at midnight in your device's timezone if a ritual was not completed that day. Premium members have one streak freeze per seven-day period — it is applied automatically when you miss a day. To check remaining freezes, open Settings → Premium.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → She Affirms → Cancel. You can also reach this from within the app: Settings → Premium → Manage subscription.

If you cancel, you keep Premium features until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again.

I paid for Lifetime but it says I am not Premium.

Open the app → Settings → Premium → tap "Restore purchases". This queries the App Store for any active purchase on your Apple ID and unlocks Premium immediately. If it still does not work after a minute, email us with the Apple ID email you used to buy (we do not see your password, only the purchase record).

Can I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, find She Affirms in your purchase history, and tap "Report a Problem". Apple decides the refund within a few days.

What data do you collect?

Almost none. The app runs on-device — your affirmations, mood entries, favorites, and streaks live in your iPhone's storage only. We do not maintain a user account or server. We use Firebase Analytics for aggregate event counts (how many people open the app each day, for example), never for anything that identifies you. The full breakdown is in our Privacy Policy.

Is the app available in my language?

At launch, She Affirms ships in English, German, Spanish, French, Portuguese (Brazil), and Turkish. Each language has its own 3,000- affirmation library — not machine-translated.

An affirmation is off / I would like to suggest one.

Email us the exact text and language, we read every message. If an affirmation lands poorly, we remove or rewrite it in the next release.
