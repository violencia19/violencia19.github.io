Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore your Premium subscription if you've previously subscribed using the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > She Affirms > Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Can I change my language?

Yes! Go to Settings in the app and select your preferred language. She Affirms is available in English, Spanish, Turkish, Portuguese, German, and French.

How does the streak work?

Complete your morning ritual (5 cards) for half a day, and your evening ritual (3 cards) for the other half. Both together count as a full day. Premium members get streak freezes to protect their progress.

How do I add a widget?

Long press on your Home Screen, tap the + button in the top corner, search for "She Affirms," and choose your preferred widget size. Lock Screen widgets are available too.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect personal information, and we don't track you. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
